Blade	-	These turn a part of the Navi into a sword! They hit hard, and some even have extra non-combat utility! Navis can **parry** with these chips.	
Hazard	-	These chips alter the environment, either by creating new obstacles, or destroying what's already there.	
Impact	-	These are melee-focused chips that do less damage, but often have a wide range of effects! Navis can **parry** with these chips.	
Rush	-	These chips tend to move your Navi, typically into Close range of your target! Downshift defense rolls if used below listed range.	
Shot	-	These chips are powerful projectiles! They can be fired beyond the listed range, but the defense roll is downshifted.	
Summon	-	These create something to assist the Navi, whether it be a tool, a specialized Virus, or even cover!	
Support	-	These chips boost the Navi's abilities, and create debilitating effects!	
Thrown	-	These chips are similar to Shot chips, these are more difficult to use but carry powerful effects!	
Wave	-	These chips create explosive and/or wide attacks. All **Close** to the target get hit too, potentially including the user.	

Artillery	-	These Viruses deal high amounts of damage at range, typically using Sense or Affinity.	
Disruption	-	These Viruses hinder Navis to make them vulnerable to other attackers.	
Striker	-	These Viruses are durable and melee-focused, needing to get up close to be a threat.	
Support	-	These Viruses aid other enemies, protecting them and boosting their abilities.	
Wrecker	-	These Viruses introduce environmental obstacles and threats.
